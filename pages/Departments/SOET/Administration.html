Message

The Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University (BGSBU) was established through Jammu & Kashmir Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University Act No. XVI of 2002 and is named after revered saint Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah. The School of Engineering and Technology (SoET) is an important constituent of BGSBU and was established in 2007 with a mission to serve the nation and society. The school offers technical education in various branches of Engineering and Technology.

It has a rich legacy of serving the nation through excellence in teaching and high-quality research, producing technocrats who shine on both national and international stages. The dynamic curriculum, evolving with industry demands, ensures students attain competence as per market requirements.

Our expert faculty integrate technical knowledge with practical implementation using modern teaching methodologies and state-of-the-art laboratories. Active participation in conferences, workshops, and faculty development programs rejuvenates their knowledge, playing a pivotal role in knowledge dissemination.

Students are invited to embark on a journey where dreams are realized, set amidst the majestic Pir-Panjaal range. We urge every student to keep their passion alive and transform themselves to achieve success.
