Prof. Jawaid Iqbal

Vice Chancellor

Dear Students, Faculty, Staff Members and Alumni,

It is with profound honour and great excitement that I assume the role of Vice-Chancellor of Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University. Our university shines with remarkable academic achievements and community engagement, and I am eager to uphold its distinguished legacy. Our university is built on values that deeply respect our cultural heritage and innovative education. We aim to develop an atmosphere where students absorb knowledge, sharpen their critical thinking, embrace integrity, and foster a deep sense of social responsibility.

Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University values diversity as a crucial part of its institutional core. Amidst this dynamic landscape, students representing a variety of backgrounds, languages, and cultural traditions join forces to cultivate an extraordinary community that prizes both collective coherence and individual identity. Our campus functions as a lively centre for education; it is an enthusiastic community that promotes enduring relationships and collective esteem.

As we transition through the rapidly changing educational landscape, we genuinely understand the significance of welcoming technological advancements and research. We are in the process of implementing the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. This ambitious policy offers a framework for holistic, multidisciplinary education, and is designed to ensure that our students are not only academically adept but also equipped with the critical thinking, creativity, and skills needed to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our inspiring alumni showcase the remarkable quality and values of our university. We proudly uphold a legacy of success for all who represent our name worldwide. Our commitment to inclusivity and superiority remains steadfast. Collectively, we can facilitate the prosperity of Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University and contribute positively to society.

Prof. Jawaid Iqbal

Vice Chancellor
